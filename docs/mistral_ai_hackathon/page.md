About Hackathon

Mistral Worldwide Hackathon
---------------------------

**Mistral AI** is launching its largest hackathon ever.

The **Mistral Worldwide Hackathon** takes place simultaneously in 7 cities:

**Paris / London / NYC / SF / Tokyo / Singapore / Sydney + online**

We’re bringing together **1,500+ AI engineers** for a **2-day overnight hackathon** around the world.

Organized in partnership with [Weights & Biases](https://wandb.ai/site/), [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/fr-fr/), [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/), Operated by the [Iterate](http://hackiterate.com/) team. Special awards sponsors include: [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/), [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/), [Jump Trading](https://www.jumptrading.com/), [WhiteCircle](https://whitecircle.ai/), [Supercell](https://supercell.com/), [Tilde Research](https://tilderesearch.com/), [Giant](https://www.giant.vc/) and [Raise](https://www.raise.co/).

Practical Info
--------------

**Dates:** February 28th – March 1st

**Schedule:** 9:00 AM → 7:00 PM (following day)

**Teams:** 1–4 people

*   Limited capacity per city
*   Overnight stay possible at all venues
*   Exact venue shared with selected participants
*   Applications reviewed on a rolling basis by Mistral
*   Breakfast, lunch, and dinner provided
*   Selected participants notified one week before the event

**Before event day:** find your teammates in advance, or we’ll help you match on-site.

See you there.
